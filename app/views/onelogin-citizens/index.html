One Login AYTQ: areas 1 and 2

ID verified journeys (area 1)

Designs for citizens signing in to AYTQ with One Login, where One Login is able to verify the citizen's identity and they pass directly to AYTQ without needing to go through service verification instead.

Know NI number → match found → connect success
No NI number → TRN known → match found → connect success
No NI number → TRN known → no match → connect fail (support request)
Know NI number → no match → dead end (no TRN)

Service verification fallback (area 2)

Designs for citizens signing in to AYTQ with One Login, when One Login is not able to verify the citizen's identity and they are sent for service verification instead.

Need more information to verify your identity → Enter name → Enter DOB → NI number → TRN known → Upload proof of your identity → no match → connect fail (support request)

Need more information to verify your identity → Enter name → Enter DOB → NI number → TRN not known → dead end
