You cannot use this service if you do not have a teacher reference number (TRN)

You usually have a TRN if you:

  • have a recognised teaching status such as qualified teacher status (QTS), early years teacher status (EYTS) or qualified teacher learning and skills (QTLS) status
  • hold or are completing a mandatory qualification
  • hold, are registering for or completing a national professional qualification (NPQ)
  • mentor trainee or early career teachers
  • have a teacher's pension
  • have a teaching-related restriction recorded in England

If none of these apply to you, you do not need to use this service.
